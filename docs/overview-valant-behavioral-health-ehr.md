# Overview: Valant Behavioral Health EHR

Valant Behavioral Health EHR is a cloud-based EHR and practice management platform built specifically for behavioral health and mental health practices. It combines clinical tools, operational workflows, and patient experience features in one system so practices can grow efficiently while maintaining high-quality care.

## Who Valant is for

- Outpatient behavioral health and mental health practices
- Group practices and multi-location organizations
- Practices running IOP/PHP or other intensive outpatient programs
- Clinical and administrative teams, including:
  - Psychiatrists, therapists, counselors, psychologists
  - Clinical directors and program managers
  - Billing teams, revenue cycle managers, and practice owners

- Purpose-built for behavioral health, not adapted from a general medical EHR
- Consolidates multiple tools into one platform
- Supports growth, multi-site operations, and program-based care
- Focus on both clinical quality and financial performance
