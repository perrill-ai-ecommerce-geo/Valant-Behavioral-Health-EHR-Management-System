### FAQ Section

**What is the difference between IOP and PHP?**

IOP (Intensive Outpatient Program) generally involves fewer hours of treatment per week compared to PHP (Partial Hospitalization Program), which offers more intensive care but still allows patients to return home at night.

**Why is specialized software necessary for IOP/PHP practices?**

Specialized software is necessary to handle the unique operational, clinical, and financial challenges of IOP/PHP practices, such as group scheduling, complex billing, and compliance with insurance requirements.

**How can I ensure the software I choose is compliant with regulations?**

Ensure the software vendor provides documentation of compliance with current regulatory standards and offers regular updates to address evolving requirements.
