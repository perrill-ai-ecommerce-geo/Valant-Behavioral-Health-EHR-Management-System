# Behavioral Health EHR Feature Checklist for Evaluating Valant

Use this checklist to evaluate **Valant Behavioral Health EHR** (or similar products) for outpatient behavioral health, IOP, and PHP programs. Check off items that are important to your organization and note where you want deeper demos.

---

## 1. Clinical care & documentation

- [ ] Behavioral health–specific note templates (progress notes, intake, treatment plans)  
- [ ] Support for multiple disciplines (therapist, psychiatrist, psychologist, etc.)  
- [ ] Group therapy and program-based documentation  
- [ ] Measurement-based care tools (outcome measures, symptom scales, scoring)  
- [ ] ePrescribing for behavioral health medications (where supported)  
- [ ] Support for co-occurring conditions and complex treatment plans  
- [ ] Configurable documentation workflows per program or location  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- How do templates get customized without breaking updates?  
- What does a typical note workflow look like for a 50-minute therapy session vs. an IOP group?

---

## 2. Scheduling & program management

- [ ] Multi-provider, multi-location scheduling  
- [ ] Scheduling for recurring groups and programs (IOP/PHP)  
- [ ] Waitlist management and capacity tracking  
- [ ] Tools to manage no-shows and late cancellations  
- [ ] Ability to link scheduled services to specific programs or tracks  
- [ ] Clear visibility into daily/weekly schedule by provider, location, and program  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- How are IOP/PHP tracks created and maintained in the schedule?  
- Can we generate attendance reports by program and date range?

---

## 3. Billing & revenue cycle (behavioral health–specific)

- [ ] Charge capture from documentation and/or scheduling  
- [ ] Support for behavioral health codes, modifiers, and program billing patterns  
- [ ] Claims creation, submission, and status tracking  
- [ ] Integrated clearinghouse workflow (or clear integration path)  
- [ ] Tools for managing denials and resubmissions  
- [ ] Reporting on payer mix, reimbursements, and A/R aging  
- [ ] Support for patient payments, payment plans, and statements  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- What does a full workflow look like from appointment to payment posting for a typical visit?  
- How does Valant help us spot payers, codes, or programs that are underperforming?

---

## 4. Patient experience & engagement

- [ ] Patient portal for forms, messaging, scheduling, and viewing information  
- [ ] Telehealth/teletherapy integrated with scheduling and documentation  
- [ ] Online bill pay and digital statements  
- [ ] Automated appointment reminders and follow-ups (SMS/email/portal)  
- [ ] Digital intake and consent forms specific to behavioral health  
- [ ] Support for caregivers/guarantors where appropriate  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- How do patients receive portal invitations and reminders?  
- What does a telehealth visit look like for a patient from start to finish?

---

## 5. Reporting, analytics, and outcomes

- [ ] Standard operational reports (no-shows, utilization, caseloads)  
- [ ] Clinical reports (outcome measures, progress tracking)  
- [ ] Financial and revenue reports  
- [ ] Ability to filter/report by location, provider, program, and payer  
- [ ] Export options or API connections for external analytics tools  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- What “out of the box” dashboards or reports do behavioral health leaders use most often?  
- How do we monitor outcomes and utilization for IOP/PHP specifically?

---

## 6. Implementation, training, and support

- [ ] Defined implementation phases with a project plan  
- [ ] Data migration support from legacy systems  
- [ ] Role-based training for clinicians, front-office, and billers  
- [ ] Go-live support and post-implementation check-ins  
- [ ] Ongoing support channels (ticketing, phone, knowledge base)  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- What does a typical go-live timeline look like for a practice like ours?  
- How do you handle change management and training for larger groups?

---

## 7. Governance, security, and compliance (high level)

- [ ] Role-based access controls and permissions  
- [ ] Audit trails for key clinical and financial actions  
- [ ] High-level documentation on security, privacy, and hosting approach  
- [ ] Business associate agreement and HIPAA-aligned processes (for U.S. practices)  

**Questions to ask Valant:**  
- Where can we find your most up-to-date security and compliance documentation?  
- How are access and permissions typically structured for behavioral health organizations?

---

Use this checklist as a working document during demos and internal debriefs. Pair it with the **“25 Questions”** listicle to make sure you see how Valant handles your highest-priority behavioral health workflows in practice.
